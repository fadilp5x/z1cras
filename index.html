Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our luxury car rental services in Kerala

What documents are required to rent a car?
To rent a car from Z1Cars, you'll need a valid driving license, proof of identity (Aadhaar card, passport, or voter ID), and a credit/debit card for security deposit. For tourists, a valid passport and international driving permit are required.
What is the minimum age requirement for renting?
The minimum age to rent a car from Z1Cars is 21 years. For premium and luxury vehicles, the minimum age requirement is 25 years. All drivers must have a valid driving license with at least one year of driving experience.
Do you offer chauffeur services?
Yes, Z1Cars offers professional chauffeur services for all our vehicles. Our chauffeurs are experienced, well-trained, and familiar with all routes across Kerala. This service is available at an additional cost.
What is your cancellation policy?
You can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the pickup time for a full refund. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before pickup will incur a cancellation fee of 50% of the rental amount.
Is fuel included in the rental price?
All our vehicles come with a full tank of fuel. You are expected to return the car with the same fuel level. If you return the car with less fuel, a refueling charge will be applied. Alternatively, you can opt for our prepaid fuel option.
Do you offer long-term rentals?
Yes, Z1Cars offers special rates for weekly and monthly rentals. Long-term rentals come with significant discounts compared to daily rates. Please contact us via WhatsApp or call for custom quotes on long-term rentals.
