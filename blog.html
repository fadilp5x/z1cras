Exploring Kerala's Scenic Routes: A Luxury Car Journey

Z1
Z1Cars Team
August 25, 2023 • 4 min read

Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," is a land of diverse landscapes ranging from serene backwaters to lush hill stations and pristine beaches. Exploring this beautiful state in a luxury car not only enhances the travel experience but also provides the comfort and flexibility to discover hidden gems at your own pace. In this guide, we'll take you through some of the most scenic routes in Kerala that are best experienced in the comfort of a premium vehicle from Z1Cars.

Coastal Drives: Kovalam to Varkala

One of the most scenic routes in Kerala, the coastal drive from Kovalam to Varkala offers breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea, pristine beaches, and charming fishing villages. This approximately 50-kilometer journey is best enjoyed in a luxury convertible or premium SUV from Z1Cars, allowing you to stop at leisure at viewpoints, beaches, and seaside cafes along the way. The route passes through the capital city of Trivandrum, where you can explore historical landmarks like the Padmanabhaswamy Temple before continuing to the cliffside beaches of Varkala. With Z1Cars luxury car rental Kerala, you can enjoy this coastal journey at your own pace, with the freedom to explore hidden gems that are often missed by conventional tourists.

Best time to drive: Early morning or late afternoon to avoid the midday heat and enjoy the stunning sunrise or sunset views over the Arabian Sea. Don't miss the opportunity to stop at the lighthouse beach in Kovalam and the Varkala cliff, which offers panoramic views of the coastline. The route is well-maintained, making it a pleasant drive for those unfamiliar with the area.

Hill Stations: Kochi to Munnar

The journey from Kochi to Munnar is a classic Kerala experience that takes you from the coastal plains to the misty hills of the Western Ghats. This approximately 130-kilometer route winds through tea plantations, spice gardens, and picturesque valleys, offering stunning views at every turn. The varying terrain demands a vehicle with excellent handling and comfort, making Z1Cars' premium SUVs and sedans the perfect choice for this journey.

As you ascend the hills, the climate becomes cooler, and the landscape transforms into a lush green paradise. With Z1Cars luxury car rental Kerala, you can stop at viewpoints like Cheeyappara and Valara waterfalls, visit tea museums, and explore spice plantations along the way. Our vehicles are equipped to handle the winding roads and occasional steep inclines, ensuring a safe and comfortable journey to one of Kerala's most beloved hill stations.

Must-visit stops along this route include the Thattekad Bird Sanctuary, Idukki Arch Dam, and the Blossom International Park in Munnar. The journey itself is as rewarding as the destination, with numerous photo opportunities and scenic viewpoints that showcase Kerala's natural beauty at its finest.

Cultural Journeys: Trivandrum to Thrissur

For those interested in exploring Kerala's rich cultural heritage, the journey from Trivandrum to Thrissur offers a fascinating glimpse into the state's artistic traditions and historical landmarks. This route takes you through the heart of Kerala, passing through towns like Kollam, Kottayam, and Ernakulam, each with its own unique cultural offerings.

Thrissur, known as the cultural capital of Kerala, is home to numerous temples, museums, and cultural centers. With Z1Cars luxury car rental Kerala, you can explore these cultural sites in comfort and style, with the flexibility to spend as much time as you wish at each location. Our knowledgeable chauffeurs can provide insights into the cultural significance of various landmarks and suggest the best times to visit festivals or cultural events along this route.

Key attractions along this route include the Padmanabhaswamy Temple in Trivandrum, the backwaters of Alappuzha, the Jewish Synagogue in Kochi, and the Vadakkunnathan Temple in Thrissur. The journey offers a perfect blend of spiritual, historical, and cultural experiences that showcase the rich diversity of Kerala's heritage.

Wildlife Expeditions: Thekkady to Wayanad

For nature enthusiasts and wildlife lovers, the journey from Thekkady to Wayanad offers an unforgettable experience through Kerala's most pristine natural habitats. This route takes you through the Periyar Tiger Reserve, dense forests, and spice plantations before reaching the wildlife sanctuaries and waterfalls of Wayanad. The terrain can be challenging in certain stretches, making Z1Cars' rugged SUVs like the Range Rover and Toyota Fortuner the ideal choice for this adventure.

With Z1Cars luxury car rental Kerala, you can enjoy the thrill of exploring these wilderness areas without compromising on comfort or safety. Our vehicles are equipped with advanced navigation systems and communication devices, ensuring you stay connected even in remote areas. Whether you're planning a wildlife safari, a trek to waterfalls, or a visit to tribal villages, our luxury vehicles provide the perfect base for your wildlife expedition in Kerala.

Notable attractions along this route include the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, Gavi Forest, Edakkal Caves, and Banasura Sagar Dam. The journey offers opportunities to spot elephants, bison, and various species of birds in their natural habitat, making it a paradise for wildlife enthusiasts and photographers.

Backwater Bliss: Alleppey to Kumarakom

No exploration of Kerala would be complete without experiencing its famous backwaters. The journey from Alleppey to Kumarakom takes you through the heart of Kerala's backwater region, offering serene views of houseboats, coconut groves, and traditional village life along the water's edge.

While the backwaters are best experienced on a houseboat, having a luxury car at your disposal allows you to explore the surrounding areas and attractions at your own pace. Z1Cars luxury car rental Kerala provides the perfect combination of land and water transportation, ensuring you can experience the best of both worlds during your Kerala vacation.

Must-visit attractions in this region include the Alleppey Beach, Pathiramanal Island, the Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, and the Arthunkal Church. The area is also famous for its Ayurvedic treatments and traditional Kerala cuisine, offering a holistic travel experience that engages all your senses.

Mountain Passes: Munnar to Ooty

For those seeking an extended mountain adventure, the journey from Munnar to Ooty (in neighboring Tamil Nadu) offers a spectacular drive through the Western Ghats. This route takes you through some of the most scenic mountain passes in South India, with breathtaking views of tea plantations, shola forests, and valleys below.

The route includes the famous Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary, the Anamalai Tiger Reserve, and the picturesque town of Coonoor before reaching Ooty. With Z1Cars luxury car rental Kerala, you can enjoy this mountain journey in comfort and style, with the assurance of a reliable vehicle that can handle the challenging terrain with ease.

Best time to undertake this journey is during the winter months (November to February) when the weather is pleasant and the views are clear. The route offers numerous opportunities for trekking, bird watching, and photography, making it a favorite among adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Quick Takeaways: Exploring Kerala's Scenic Routes

  • Kerala offers diverse scenic routes, from coastal drives to mountain passes, each showcasing the state's natural beauty and cultural richness.
  • Luxury car rentals from Z1Cars provide the comfort, flexibility, and safety needed to fully enjoy these scenic journeys.
  • Each route has its own unique attractions, from beaches and backwaters to hill stations and wildlife sanctuaries.
  • The best time to explore these routes varies, with the winter months (November to February) generally offering the most pleasant weather.
  • Z1Cars' premium fleet is well-equipped to handle the diverse terrains found across Kerala's scenic routes.
  • Professional chauffeurs with local knowledge can enhance your journey by providing insights and recommendations along the way.
  • Planning your route in advance and allowing time for spontaneous stops ensures you make the most of your scenic drive through Kerala.
